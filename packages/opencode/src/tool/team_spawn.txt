Spawn a non-blocking teammate agent as part of the current team.
The teammate runs as a background child session with its own agent type, prompt, and permissions.
The spawn returns immediately with the teammate's session ID and metadata.
The teammate receives team context and its final result is posted back to the lead automatically.
Teammates are instructed to send proactive kickoff, progress, handoff, and blocker updates through the team mailbox.
The lead receives automatic started/waiting updates, and active dependency teammates are notified when someone is waiting on their work.
Use this when you need multiple agents working in parallel on different aspects of the team's goal.
Use depends_on or wait_for when this teammate must wait for one or more teammate names/session IDs to complete before starting.

After spawning all needed teammates, check team_get_messages ONCE. If no messages need your attention, provide a status summary to the user and end your turn. You will be automatically woken when teammates send you messages — DO NOT poll team_get_messages in a loop.
