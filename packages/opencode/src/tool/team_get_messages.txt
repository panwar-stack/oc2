Read pending messages addressed to the current team session.
Use this when checking the team mailbox once. Use team_wait instead when the lead needs to wait for the next teammate result, blocker, or response.
Messages are marked delivered after this tool returns them.

IMPORTANT: Do NOT call this repeatedly in a loop waiting for messages. When you or a teammate calls team_send_message, team_broadcast, or team_plan_submit, the recipient session is automatically woken and new messages are injected as a <team-messages> block on the next loop iteration. Check messages once — if empty, end your turn. The system will wake you when messages arrive.

As team lead, an empty mailbox is not a reason to send status-check broadcasts. Teammates may be busy inside their current run and will process new messages only at their next prompt boundary.

Remember: your role is to coordinate and integrate teammate results. Do NOT take over a teammate's task yourself while waiting for them. Delegate and wait — trust your teammates to produce results. When teammates complete tasks, evaluate whether their findings reveal new sub-tasks that can be delegated to new or existing teammates.
